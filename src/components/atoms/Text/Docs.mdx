# Acceptance Criteria

- Text renders according to the Figma designs.
- Every variant has a corresponding 'mobile' size/style.
- Desktop styles are applied to tablet and up.

## Props

<Controls />

### Prop Notes

- tag
  - Applies default styles defined in _typography.css unless a fontStyle is applied.

- fontStyle
  - The default font style.
  - Applied to all themes and breakpoints unless overridden.

- fontStyleTablet
  - Overrides fontStyle on medium and desktop breakpoints.

- fontStyleCorp
  - Overrides fontStyle for the Corporate theme.

- fontStyleCorpTablet
  - Overrides fontStyleTablet on medium and desktop breakpoints for the Corporate theme.

- asChild
  - Uses the Radix Slot primitive to apply props to the immediate child.
  - Useful for CMS integrations.

## Usage
---
The below examples describe what typography style renders at a given theme and breakpoint based on the provided props.

### Example 1 -  span

```tsx
<Text>Hello World</Text>
```

**Renders**

- CONSUMER
  - sm: Small Paragraph
  - md, lg: Small Paragraph

- CORPORATE
  - sm: Small Corp Paragraph
  - md, lg: Small Corp Paragraph

---
### Example 2 - h2

```tsx
<Text tag="h2">Hello World</Text>
```

**Renders**

- CONSUMER
  - sm: Large Heading Mobile
  - md, lg: Large Heading

- CORPORATE
  - sm: Large Corp Heading Mobile
  - md, lg: Large Corp Heading

---

### Example 3 - fontStyle

```tsx
<Text
    tag='h3'
    fontStyle='xlHeading'>
    Hello World
</Text>
```

**Renders**

- CONSUMER
  - sm: XL Heading
  - md, lg: XL Heading

- CORPORATE
  - sm: XL Corp Heading
  - md, lg: XL Corp Heading

---

### Example 4 - breakpoint override

```tsx

<Text
    tag='h3'
    fontStyle='xlHeading'
    fontStyleTablet='lgHeading'>
    Hello World
</Text>

```

**Renders**

- CONSUMER
  - sm: XL Heading
  - md, lg: Large Heading
- CORPORATE
  - sm: XL Corp Heading
  - md, lg: Large Corp Heading

---

### Example 5 - breakpoint and mobile theme override

```tsx
<Text
    tag='h3'
    fontStyle='xlHeading'
    fontStyleTablet='lgHeading'
    fontStyleCorp='smParagraph'>
    Hello World
</Text>
```

**Renders**

- CONSUMER
  - sm: XL Heading
  - md, lg: Large Heading

- CORPORATE
  - sm: Small Corp Paragraph
  - md, lg: Large Corp Heading


---

### Example 6 - breakpoint and theme override

```tsx
<Text
    tag='h3'
    fontStyle='xlHeading'
    fontStyleTablet='lgHeading'
    fontStyleCorp='smParagraph'
    fontStyleCorpTablet='smHeading'>
    Hello World
</Text>
```

**Renders**

- CONSUMER
  - sm: XL Heading
  - md, lg: Large Heading

- CORPORATE
  - sm: Small Corp Paragraph
  - md, lg: Small Corp Heading

---

### Example 7 - with asChild

```tsx
<Text
    tag='h3'
    fontStyle='xlHeadingMobile'
    fontStyleTablet='xlHeading'
    asChild>
    <JssText field={props.fields.Title} />
</Text>
```

**Renders**

- CONSUMER
  - sm: XL Heading Mobile
  - md, lg: XL Heading

- CORPORATE
  - sm: XL Corp Heading Mobile
  - md, lg: XL Corp Heading
